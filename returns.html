Refunds & Returns

At HerAlways, each piece of jewelry is personalized and crafted especially for you.

Return Policy

Due to the custom nature of our products, returns and exchanges are generally not accepted once an order has been processed. However, we aim to ensure customer satisfaction and may review requests on a case-by-case basis.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your item arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with clear photo evidence. We will gladly arrange a replacement or appropriate resolution.

Order Issues

If there is an error in customization caused by us, we will provide a replacement at no additional cost.

Important Notes

  • Personalized items are generally non-returnable unless damaged or incorrect
  • Return requests may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis

For assistance, please contact us at support@heralways.com.
